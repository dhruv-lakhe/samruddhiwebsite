About Our Society

Trusted Cooperative Banking Since 2010

Established on 22nd November 2010 by Ma. Shri. Vasant Sampatirav Nagare, Samruddhi Multi State Co-operative Credit Society Ltd. has been dedicated to providing accessible, transparent, and member-focused financial services to the community of Dharashiv (Osmanabad), Maharashtra.

As a registered multi-state cooperative credit society, we promote thrift, self-help, and mutual aid among our members while supporting local economic development through affordable credit and secure savings options.

Competitive interest rates on deposits and loans

Minimal documentation and quick loan processing

Member-driven governance and transparent operations
